History

The commpany opened in 1918,Spring. The first owner of this company is Rustam Qodirov. He firstly produced only wafers with chocolate with his 4 employees. His wafers got very famous all among Asia.

After the demand for his wafers increased significantly, Rustam Qodirov had to hire more employees and so his company continued to get bigger

As so many people from around the asia wanted his wafers, Rustam solved this by opening filials. Now there are 5 filials of the company:
Tashkent
Khorezm
Astana
Samarqand
Pekin

Today

Today the "KREMBER" commpany is owned by Jamshid Abdullaev, and the company is prpoducing more than 20 types of candies.
